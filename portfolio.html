A frontend engineer, some of the time.

Luciano · 2026

I've spent the last six years writing the small, careful code that turns design into something you can actually click — most of it for eCommerce.

I started as a junior at a Tirana agency, became Team Leader at Eurostep SRL, and most recently led frontend work at Exposure, a UK BigCommerce specialist. Along the way I've built custom checkouts, re-platformed stores, mentored developers, and integrated more third-party services than I'd care to admit.

These days I'm split between two things I love: shipping serious commerce frontends, and figuring out where AI actually belongs inside them. Outside of code, I'm easily found near coffee, espadrilles, or a half-finished side project.
